    <section id="recipe">
        <h2>Easy Samosa Recipe</h2>
        <h3>Ingredients (for 10-12 samosas):</h3>
        <ul>
            <li>2 cups boiled potatoes, mashed</li>
            <li>1 cup peas</li>
            <li>1 tsp cumin seeds, 1 tsp garam masala, salt to taste</li>
            <li>1 tbsp oil, chopped green chilies, ginger-garlic paste</li>
            <li>Samosa wrappers or homemade dough</li>
            <li>Oil for frying</li>
        </ul>
        <h3>Steps:</h3>
        <ol>
            <li>Heat oil, add cumin, ginger-garlic paste, and spices. Add potatoes and peas; cook for 5-7 mins.</li>
            <li>Fill wrappers into triangles, seal edges with water.</li>
            <li>Fry in hot oil until golden. Serve with chutney!</li>
        </ol>
        <p>Prep time: 20 mins | Cook time: 15 mins | Total: 35 mins</p>
